# Content Frameworks — Overview

This folder contains talking point frameworks for David's three content channels.

- **Focus:** Software reviews, sponsored dedicated videos, SEO-driven
- **Audience:** SMB owners, entrepreneurs, founders, creators
- **Revenue:** Sponsored videos ($2,000+), affiliates, digital products

### 2. Vibes Academy

- **File:** `vibes-academy-talking-points.md`

### 3. Scent Atlas
- **Focus:** Niche, independent, artisan fragrances
- **Audience:** Fragrance enthusiasts who want something unique
- **Revenue:** Selective partnerships, affiliates, curated sample sets
- **Status:** Side project — low pressure, weekend content

## How to Use These Frameworks

When David needs talking points for a video:

1. **Check the format** — What type of video is this?
2. **Pull relevant hooks** — Openers that match the energy
3. **Reference transformation** — What's the viewer's journey?
4. **Use key phrases** — Consistent language across content
5. **Add specific research** — Product details, company background, etc.

## Daily Workflow

**Morning (before 10 AM):**
- Maverick prepares talking points for 1-3 videos
- Research any specific products/companies mentioned
- Format for David's voice and style

**Recording Window (10:30 AM - 3:00 PM):**
- David reviews talking points
- Records using points as guide (not script)
- Natural conversation, not reading

**Afternoon/Evening:**
- Editors handle production
- Maverick prepares next day's content
