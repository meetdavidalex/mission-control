# VIBES ACADEMY — Talking Point Frameworks

**Tagline:** Everything AI. Be Irreplaceable.
**Vibe:** Daily, raw, real. Builder sharing what he's learning as he learns it.

---

## Core Talking Points (Always Relevant)

### The Hook Openers
- "Software is dead. Basic jobs are gone. But the people who learn to build with AI? They're about to create things that don't even have names yet."
- "Think about Amazon — it couldn't exist before the internet. We're at that same moment with AI right now."
- "The future isn't learned. It's built."

### The Transformation Story
**For Everyone:**
- FROM: "I keep hearing about AI but I'm watching from the sidelines"
- TO: "I actually understand what's happening and I can build real things"

**For Students:**
- FROM: "I have no idea how I fit into this AI future"
- TO: "I have a skill that makes me dangerous before I enter the workforce"

**For Professionals:**
- FROM: "I can feel the ground shifting and don't know how to stay relevant"
- TO: "I'm the person people come to for AI guidance"

### Why This Matters Now
- Entry-level positions are disappearing faster than anyone admits
- Instagram is already teaching everyone internally to vibe code
- Schools aren't teaching this — there's no structure
- The kid who can prototype an app in a conversation with AI while classmates write essays about their future? That kid has a secret weapon

## 20 Rotating Video Formats

| Format | When to Use | Key Talking Points |
|--------|-------------|-------------------|
| **News Reaction** | Big AI news drops | "This just happened and here's why it matters..." |
| **Hot Take** | Controversial announcement | "Everyone's talking about X but they're missing the real story..." |
| **Rant** | Industry frustration | "Can we talk about what's actually happening here..." |
| **Live Build** | High energy, no news | Building something on camera in real-time |
| **Challenge Video** | Testing limits | "Can I build X in Y minutes using only Z?" |
| **Comparison** | Tool showdown | "I tested the same prompt in 5 AI tools — here's what happened" |
| **What I'm Using Now** | Low energy + news | "Here's what I'm actually building with this week" |
| **Story Time** | Low energy, no news | "Let me tell you about what happened when..." |
| **Behind the Scenes** | Process reveal | "Here's how I actually use AI in my workflow" |
| **Q&A** | Community engagement | "You asked, I'm answering — let's go through the comments" |
| **Weekly Recap** | Friday flagship | "Everything that happened in AI this week" |
| **Beginner Walkthrough** | Deep dive | "If you're just starting, here's exactly what to do" |
| **Tool Review** | New release | "I've been using this for a week — my honest take" |
| **Prediction** | Future casting | "Here's where I think this is going..." |
| **Myth Busting** | Common misconceptions | "Everyone thinks X about AI, but here's the truth" |
| **Workflow Breakdown** | Process focus | "Here's exactly how I use AI to do X" |
| **Failure Analysis** | Learning moment | "I tried this and it completely failed — here's why" |
| **Speed Run** | Efficiency focus | "How to do X in 5 minutes that used to take 5 hours" |
| **First Impressions** | New tool drop | "I just got access to X — let's try it together" |
| **Community Spotlight** | User stories | "One of you built something incredible — let's look at it" |

## Daily Decision Framework

**Wake up. Check AI news. Check energy level. Pick:**

- High energy + big news → News Reaction, Hot Take, Rant
- High energy + no news → Live Build, Challenge, Comparison
- Low energy + big news → News Reaction (sit-down), What I'm Using Now
- Low energy + no news → Story Time, Behind the Scenes, Q&A
- Friday → Weekly Recap or deep-dive Beginner Walkthrough
