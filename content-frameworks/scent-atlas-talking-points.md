# SCENT ATLAS — Talking Point Frameworks

**Tagline:** Your signature scent is out there. You just haven't found it yet.
**Vibe:** Anthony Bourdain for fragrance — journey and story over review

---

## Core Talking Points (Always Relevant)

### The Hook Openers
- "You've been wearing the same fragrances as everyone else. Not because you chose them — because that's all you were shown."
- "There's an entire world of fragrance most people never discover."
- "Every bottle has a story. Every scent has an origin."
- "Your signature scent is out there. You just haven't found it yet."

### The Transformation Story
- FROM: "I wear whatever's popular because I don't know what else is out there"
- TO: "I have fragrances with real stories that nobody in the room has smelled, and people ask me about it everywhere I go"

**Emotional Core:** Going from invisible and generic to being noticed. That moment someone asks "what are you wearing?" — that's the payoff.

### The Origin Story (David's Journey)
- Left mainstream fragrance world wanting something unique
- Didn't want to smell like everybody else
- Discovered a world beyond the department store counter
- Started traveling specifically to find hidden gem fragrance houses
- Same journey he's inviting the audience to take

---

## What This Channel Is NOT

- NOT a channel that reviews everything sent to it
- NOT pay-to-play sponsored fragrance-of-the-week
- NOT mass-market hype
- NOT competing with designer release review channels
- NOT about note breakdowns and performance ratings

**The Selectivity Promise:** When Scent Atlas features something, it means something. David only reviews what he personally wears and loves.

## 10 Rotating Video Formats

| Format | Focus | Key Elements |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| **Discovery Vlog** | Travel + fragrance | Finding a house in a new country, meeting the perfumer |
| **Story Behind the Bottle** | Deep narrative | The origin, the creator, the journey of how this scent came to be |

| **House Deep Dive** | Brand exploration | Everything about one specific niche house |
| **The Journey** | Personal story | How David discovered a specific fragrance or house |

## Key Differentiators to Mention

1. **Selectivity over volume** — Only features fragrances David personally wears
2. **Story over specs** — Origin, perfumer, journey matter more than note breakdowns
3. **Exploration over expertise** — Curious traveler, not world's foremost expert
4. **Niche focus** — Independent, artisan, small houses, minimal synthetics
5. **Global perspective** — Finding fragrances in different countries and cultures

## Revenue Streams

1. **Selective Brand Partnerships** — Exclusivity makes partnerships worth more
2. **Affiliate Links** — Niche fragrances = higher price points = higher commissions
3. **Curated Sample Sets** — "The Scent Atlas Collection" — try before committing $200+
4. **Ad Revenue** — Luxury/lifestyle niche with strong CPMs
5. **Travel Content Sponsorship** — Tourism boards, airlines, hotels, regional fragrance houses

## The Bourdain Approach

**What to emulate:**
- Didn't review every restaurant
- Took you on a journey
- Made you care about the people and place behind the food
- The food was never the point — the story was the point

**For Scent Atlas:**
- The bottle is never the point
- The story is the point
- The person who made it is the point
- The place you found it is the point
